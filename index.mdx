## Get Help in 3 Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open ZipDrop and pin your location">
    The app automatically detects where you are. Confirm your location or drag the pin to your exact position on the road.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the type of help you need">
    Select from flat tyre, battery jump-start, fuel delivery, lockout, towing, or general breakdown. Add a note to help your mechanic prepare.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track your mechanic in real time">
    A verified mechanic accepts your request and heads to you. Watch them approach on the live map and chat directly in the app.
  </Step>
</Steps>
