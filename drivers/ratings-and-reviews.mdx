After every completed job, ZipDrop asks you to rate your mechanic and, if you'd like, leave a written review. Your feedback is one of the most important tools ZipDrop has for keeping the platform safe and high-quality — it helps great mechanics stand out, flags any issues that need investigating, and gives future customers confidence when choosing who to trust. The whole process takes less than a minute, and you can always edit or add to your review later if you change your mind.

## How ratings work

You rate your mechanic on a scale of **1 to 5 stars**, where 5 is excellent. Alongside the star rating, you can choose from a set of quick-tap feedback tags and write a free-text review in your own words. Your review is published on the mechanic's profile after it passes ZipDrop's moderation check (see the note below). Your name and first initial appear alongside your review; your full surname is never shown publicly.

## Rating your mechanic

The rating prompt appears automatically as soon as your mechanic marks the job as complete. You don't need to navigate anywhere — just respond to the prompt when it appears on screen.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Respond to the rating prompt">
    As soon as the job is marked complete, a rating screen slides up automatically in the app. If you dismiss it, you can return to it at any time via **Profile → Job History → select the job → Leave a Review**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your star rating">
    Tap a star between 1 and 5 to reflect your overall experience. The stars are labelled to guide you: 1 = Poor, 3 = Okay, 5 = Excellent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose quick-feedback tags (optional)">
    Select any tags that apply to your experience. Examples include:
    - **Arrived quickly**
    - **Very professional**
    - **Solved the issue**
    - **Clearly explained the problem**
    - **Great value**
    - **Went the extra mile**

    You can select as many tags as you like, or skip this step entirely.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write a review (optional)">
    Tap the text field and write a free-text review in your own words. There's no minimum length — even a short sentence helps. Be specific about what went well or what could have been better; detailed reviews are the most useful to other customers and to mechanics trying to improve.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap 'Submit'">
    Tap the **Submit** button to send your rating. You'll see a confirmation message and your review will enter ZipDrop's moderation queue before being published.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  ZipDrop moderates all reviews before they go live. Reviews are checked automatically and by the support team for inappropriate language, personal information, and content that violates ZipDrop's community guidelines. This usually takes less than an hour. If your review is not published, you'll receive a notification explaining why.
</Note>

## Can I edit a review?

Yes. You can edit any review within **48 hours** of submitting it. After that window closes, reviews are locked to maintain the integrity of the ratings system.

To edit a review:
1. Go to **Profile → Job History**
2. Select the job whose review you want to change
3. Tap **Edit Review**
4. Make your changes and tap **Submit** again

Your updated review re-enters the moderation queue and will be published once it clears.

## Reporting a mechanic

If you experienced a serious issue during a job — such as unprofessional behaviour, damage to your vehicle, dishonest pricing, or anything that made you feel unsafe — you should report it directly to ZipDrop rather than, or as well as, leaving a review.

You can report a mechanic in two ways:

- **From a review:** Tap the **flag icon** next to any review on a mechanic's profile to report it or the associated job directly.
- **From Job History:** Go to **Profile → Job History**, select the relevant job, and tap **Report Issue**. Describe what happened in as much detail as you can. ZipDrop's support team will investigate and follow up with you within 24 hours.

<Tip>
  The more detail you include in a report, the faster the support team can act. Include the date and time of the job, what was said or done, and any photos if relevant — you can attach images directly from the Report Issue screen.
</Tip>

## Your own driver rating

Mechanics on ZipDrop can also rate you after a job is complete, on the same 1–5 star scale. Your driver rating is shown to mechanics when a new request comes in, and a lower rating can mean mechanics take slightly longer to accept your jobs.

Here are a few simple ways to keep your rating high:

- **Be in a safe, accessible location** when your mechanic arrives, or communicate clearly if you had to move.
- **Be ready with your vehicle details** — make, model, registration, and a description of the fault — so your mechanic can prepare.
- **Respond promptly to in-app messages** from your mechanic while they're on their way.
- **Be courteous and patient** — your mechanic is there to help, and a friendly interaction goes a long way.

You can view your current driver rating at any time by going to **Profile → My Rating**.
