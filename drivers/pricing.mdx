With ZipDrop, you always see a price estimate before you confirm a request — there are no hidden fees added after the fact and no surprise bills when the job is done. Every quote is built from a clear set of factors so you understand exactly what you're paying for. If your situation changes during the job (for example, a tyre repair turns into a full replacement), any difference in parts cost is shown as a separate line item and confirmed with you before it's charged.

## How Your Price Estimate Is Calculated

ZipDrop generates your upfront estimate by combining four components:

**1. Service type base rate**
Each type of breakdown assistance has a fixed starting price that reflects the skill and equipment required. A battery jump-start, for instance, has a lower base rate than a tow, which requires a specialist vehicle.

**2. Distance / travel surcharge**
If the nearest available mechanic is more than **5 miles** from your location, a travel surcharge is added to cover their additional journey time and fuel. This surcharge is calculated at the time of your request and is always included in the upfront estimate you see — you won't be charged extra later.

**3. Time of day**
Demand for roadside assistance varies throughout the day. ZipDrop applies the following time-based pricing adjustments:

- **Peak hours:** 7–9 am and 5–8 pm (Monday–Friday) — higher demand means prices may be slightly elevated.
- **Late night:** 11 pm–6 am — a late-night rate applies to account for unsociable hours and reduced mechanic availability.

Outside these windows, standard pricing applies.

**4. Parts required**
If your job requires consumable parts — such as a replacement tyre, a can of fuel, or jump-start equipment — the estimated cost of those parts is shown as a **separate line item** in your quote. This keeps the labour and parts costs transparent, and means you can see exactly what you're paying for each element of the service.

<Note>

Your final price is confirmed after the job is complete if any parts used differ from the original quote — for example, if your tyre size requires a higher-specification replacement. ZipDrop will always notify you of any adjustment before applying it.
</Note>

## Surge Pricing

During periods of unusually high demand — such as severe weather, rush hours, or local events — ZipDrop may apply surge pricing to help ensure mechanics are available when you need them most. When surge pricing is active, you'll see a clear surge indicator on the pricing screen before you confirm your request.

You are never charged a surged rate without seeing it first. If the surge price doesn't work for you, you can cancel at no cost before confirming (see Cancellation Fees below). Surge pricing is temporary; if you're able to wait, prices typically return to normal within 20–40 minutes.

<Tip>
  During high-demand periods, accept your quote promptly once you're comfortable with the price. Quotes are held for 60 seconds — if you wait too long, the estimate may be refreshed at the current (potentially higher) rate.
</Tip>

## Cancellation Fees

Sometimes plans change, and ZipDrop understands that. Here's how cancellation fees work:

- **Within 2 minutes of requesting:** Cancel for free. No charge applied.
- **After a mechanic is en route:** A **£5 cancellation fee** applies to compensate the mechanic for their time and travel. This is charged to your default payment method.
- **After job completion:** Once a mechanic has marked a job as complete, the full job cost applies and standard cancellation no longer applies. Use the dispute process below if you believe there's been an error.

## Disputed Charges

If you believe you've been charged incorrectly — for example, if you were charged for parts you didn't receive, or if the final price differs from what you expected — ZipDrop's support team is here to help.

To raise a dispute, go to **Profile → Job History**, tap the relevant job, and select **Report Issue**. Choose the reason that best describes your concern and provide any additional details. The support team will review your case and respond within **24 hours**. If your dispute is upheld, a refund is issued to your original payment method within 3–5 business days.

You can also contact support directly at **support@zipdrop.co.uk** if you prefer to reach out by email.
