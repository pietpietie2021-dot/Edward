ZipDrop makes paying for roadside assistance as stress-free as possible — the last thing you need when your car breaks down is a complicated checkout process. You can store multiple payment methods in the app and switch between them at any time, so you're always ready to request help the moment you need it.

## Accepted Payment Methods

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Credit & Debit Card" icon="credit-card">
    Pay with any Visa, Mastercard, or American Express card. Your card details are stored securely and never shared with the attending mechanic.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Apple Pay" icon="apple">
    Available on iOS devices. Use Face ID, Touch ID, or your passcode to authorise payment instantly — no card number required.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Google Pay" icon="google">
    Available on Android devices. Confirm payment with your fingerprint or PIN using any card already linked to your Google account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="ZipDrop Wallet" icon="wallet">
    Top up your in-app balance in advance and check out in a single tap. Ideal if you want to keep roadside cover budgeted and ready to go.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Pay on Arrival (Cash)

In some areas, ZipDrop offers a **Pay on Arrival** option, allowing you to pay the mechanic directly in cash when the job is complete. You'll see this option at checkout if it's available in your location. This method is not available everywhere, so always keep a digital payment method saved as a backup.

## Adding a Payment Method

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Payment Methods">
    Tap your profile photo in the top-right corner of the app, then select **Payment Methods** from the menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap 'Add Payment Method'">
    Tap the **+ Add Payment Method** button at the bottom of the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a type and enter your details">
    Choose your preferred method — card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, or ZipDrop Wallet top-up. Follow the on-screen prompts to enter your card number, expiry date, and CVV, or authenticate via your device wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and save">
    Tap **Save** to store your payment method securely. It will appear in your Payment Methods list immediately and is ready to use on your next request.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Setting a Default Payment Method

Your default payment method is automatically selected at checkout each time you request a service. To change it, go to **Profile → Payment Methods**, tap the method you want to use as your default, and select **Set as Default**. A checkmark will appear next to it to confirm your choice. You can still switch to a different method at the checkout screen on any individual request.

---

## Removing a Payment Method

To remove a saved payment method, go to **Profile → Payment Methods**, swipe left on the card or method you want to delete, and tap **Remove**. If the method you're removing is your current default, ZipDrop will ask you to choose a new default before completing the removal.

<Warning>
  You cannot remove a payment method if it is the only one saved to your account and you have an active or pending job. Add a replacement method first, then remove the old one.
</Warning>

## ZipDrop Wallet

The ZipDrop Wallet is a prepaid balance held within the app. It's a convenient way to keep funds ready for roadside assistance without needing to enter card details at the time of a breakdown.

### Topping Up Your Wallet

To add funds, go to **Profile → Payment Methods → ZipDrop Wallet** and tap **Top Up**. Enter the amount you'd like to add — the **minimum top-up is £5** — and confirm using your saved card or device wallet. The funds are added to your balance instantly.

### Key Wallet Details

- **Minimum top-up:** £5
- **Maximum balance:** £500
- **Balance expiry:** Your ZipDrop Wallet balance never expires. Any amount you add remains available until you spend it, regardless of how long it sits in your account.
- **Refunds:** If a job is cancelled, any Wallet funds charged are returned to your Wallet balance within minutes.

---

<Note>
  ZipDrop does not charge your payment method when you submit a request. Payment is only taken once the mechanic has marked the job as complete, giving you time to confirm everything has been resolved to your satisfaction.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Always keep at least one valid, active payment method saved to your account. If your saved card has expired or your Wallet balance is insufficient, your request may fail to process — not something you want to discover at the roadside.
</Tip>
