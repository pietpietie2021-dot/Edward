When you break down, every minute counts. ZipDrop connects you with a nearby qualified mechanic in just a few taps — no phone queues, no waiting on hold, and no guessing when help will arrive. Open the app, confirm your location, and a mechanic can be on their way to you in under two minutes.

## Requesting assistance

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open ZipDrop and confirm your location">
    When you open the app, ZipDrop automatically places a pin on your current GPS location. Check that the pin matches where you actually are — for example, the correct side of a dual carriageway or the right entrance to a car park. Drag the pin to adjust it if needed, or type an address or landmark into the search bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the type of help you need">
    Tap the service that best describes your situation — flat tyre, battery jump-start, fuel delivery, lockout, towing, or general breakdown. Not sure? Choose **General Breakdown** and your mechanic will diagnose the problem when they arrive. You can find a full description of each service type on the [Job Types](/drivers/job-types) page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add notes for your mechanic (optional)">
    Use the notes field to give your mechanic any helpful details before they set off. For example: *'nearside front tyre'*, *'car is a red hatchback in bay 14'*, or *'gate code is 1234'*. The more context you provide, the faster they can get to work when they arrive.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your upfront price estimate">
    ZipDrop shows you a fixed price estimate before you confirm your request. This price covers the standard call-out and labour for your chosen service. If additional parts or work are required on-site, your mechanic will let you know before carrying out any extra work.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your request">
    Tap **Confirm Request** to submit. ZipDrop instantly notifies all available mechanics in your area. You don't need to do anything else — sit tight and watch for an acceptance notification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for a mechanic to accept">
    A nearby mechanic will typically accept your request within two minutes. Once someone accepts, you'll receive a notification and be taken straight to the live tracking screen, where you can see their location, estimated arrival time, and contact them directly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Accurate location information is critical — if your pin is in the wrong place, your mechanic may head to the wrong spot and your arrival time will increase significantly. Always double-check the pin position before confirming, especially on motorways, multi-storey car parks, or large retail sites where GPS can drift.
</Warning>

## What happens after you confirm?

Once you tap **Confirm Request**, ZipDrop's matching system broadcasts your job to qualified mechanics in your area who are currently available. Mechanics can see your location, the service you need, and any notes you've added — they accept based on their proximity and readiness.

The first mechanic to accept your request is assigned to your job. You'll see their name, photo, vehicle details, star rating, and an estimated arrival time the moment they confirm. ZipDrop monitors the route in real time, so your ETA updates automatically as your mechanic travels.

If more than one mechanic is interested, ZipDrop always assigns the one best placed to reach you quickly. You don't need to compare or choose — the app handles it for you.

## Cancelling a request

You can cancel your request at any time from the **My Request** screen by tapping **Cancel Request**.

- **Within 2 minutes of confirming:** Cancellation is completely free, no questions asked.
- **After 2 minutes (before a mechanic accepts):** Cancellation is still free.
- **After a mechanic has accepted your request:** A small cancellation fee applies to fairly compensate the mechanic for the time and travel already committed. The exact fee is shown on the cancellation confirmation screen before you proceed.

To cancel, tap the **⋯** menu on the tracking screen and select **Cancel Request**. You'll be asked to confirm before any charge is applied.

<Note>
  If no mechanics are available in your area right now, ZipDrop will let you know on the confirmation screen. You can choose to leave your request open — as soon as a mechanic becomes available nearby, they'll be notified automatically. Alternatively, tap **Get Help Another Way** to see contact details for local garages and national breakdown providers who may be able to assist.
</Note>
