Whether you're using ZipDrop for the first time or you're a regular customer, you're bound to have questions. Below you'll find answers to the things drivers ask us most often, grouped by topic so you can find what you need quickly. If your question isn't covered here, you can reach the ZipDrop support team at any time through the app or at [support@zipdrop.app](mailto:support@zipdrop.app).

## Requesting help

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What if no mechanic is available near me?">
    ZipDrop automatically widens the search radius if no mechanic is immediately available in your area. The app will keep searching and notify you of any updates as they happen. If no mechanic becomes available within 30 minutes, you'll receive a notification so you can decide whether to keep waiting or make alternative arrangements. In that situation, ZipDrop will never charge you anything.

    In areas with lower mechanic coverage, you may want to also contact your breakdown insurance provider as a backup while ZipDrop continues searching.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long does it take for a mechanic to arrive?">
    The average arrival time on ZipDrop is **15 to 25 minutes** from the moment a mechanic accepts your job. However, actual times vary depending on your location, the time of day, and current demand in your area. You can track your mechanic's live location on the map screen the moment they accept, so you'll always know exactly how far away they are and when to expect them.

    During peak times — such as icy mornings or bank holiday weekends — arrival times may be longer. The app will show you a live ETA that updates as your mechanic travels.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I request help for someone else's vehicle?">
    Yes. When you create a new job request, you'll be asked to enter the vehicle details (make, model, registration, and colour). You can enter the details of any vehicle, not just your own. This is useful if you're calling on behalf of a family member, a colleague, or a friend who doesn't have access to a smartphone.

    The job is still tied to your ZipDrop account and billed to your payment method, and you remain the point of contact for the mechanic throughout the job.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if my phone runs out of battery while I'm waiting?">
    Before you find yourself in this situation, we recommend saving the ZipDrop emergency support number — **0800 123 4567** — in your phone contacts so you can call even without the app.

    If your phone dies after a mechanic has already accepted your job, don't worry: your mechanic can still see your last known location in their app and will travel to that point. They'll also attempt to call your number. If they can't reach you and can't locate you, ZipDrop support will follow up. Keeping a phone charger or power bank in your car is always a good idea.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Payments

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When am I charged?">
    You are only charged **after the job is marked complete** by your mechanic. ZipDrop does not take any payment upfront. Your saved payment method is charged automatically once both you and your mechanic confirm the work is done. You'll receive a detailed receipt by email and in the app straight away.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if the final cost is higher than the estimate?">
    The price you see when you book is an estimate based on the fault you described. If your mechanic discovers that the work required is more involved than expected, they must notify you through the app before doing any additional work. You'll receive an updated quote and you have to **approve it in-app** before anything proceeds.

    No additional charge can be applied to your job without your explicit approval. If you're ever charged for work you didn't approve, contact support immediately via **Profile → Job History → Report Issue**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    Yes. If you're not satisfied with the way a job was completed — for example, the fault wasn't resolved, the mechanic didn't arrive, or something went wrong — contact ZipDrop support within **7 days** of the job date to request a refund.

    You can raise a refund request via **Profile → Job History → Report Issue**, or by emailing [support@zipdrop.app](mailto:support@zipdrop.app). Include your job reference number and a description of the issue. The support team will review the job details and respond within 2 business days. Refunds, where approved, are returned to your original payment method within 5–10 working days.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a subscription option?">
    Yes — **ZipDrop Plus** is ZipDrop's monthly subscription plan, available for **£9.99 per month**. As a ZipDrop Plus member you get:

    - **Reduced rates** on every job — typically 15–20% less than standard pricing
    - **Priority matching** — your job requests are shown to mechanics first, meaning faster acceptance and shorter wait times
    - **Free safety check** once per month (a 20-point vehicle inspection carried out by your mechanic at the end of any job)
    - **No surge pricing** — your rates never increase during high-demand periods
    - **Dedicated support queue** — jump to the front of the support queue if you ever need help

    You can sign up for ZipDrop Plus at any time via **Profile → ZipDrop Plus**. You can cancel your subscription at any time and you'll retain your Plus benefits until the end of the current billing period.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account management

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I change my registered vehicle?">
    To update a vehicle on your account:

    1. Go to **Profile → Vehicles**
    2. Tap the vehicle you want to edit
    3. Tap **Edit** and update the relevant details (make, model, registration, colour, or fuel type)
    4. Tap **Save**

    Your changes take effect immediately and will be used on your next job request. You can also add a new vehicle or remove one you no longer own from the same screen.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I have multiple vehicles on one account?">
    Yes. You can add up to **5 vehicles** to a single ZipDrop account. This is ideal if you have more than one car at home, own a van as well as a car, or want to be able to request help for a family member's vehicle without re-entering the details each time.

    To add a vehicle, go to **Profile → Vehicles → Add Vehicle** and fill in the details. When you create a new job request, you'll be prompted to choose which vehicle the job is for.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I delete my account?">
    You can delete your ZipDrop account at any time by going to **Profile → Settings → Delete Account**. You'll be asked to confirm your decision and provide an optional reason.

    Once you confirm deletion:
    - Your account and personal details are **scheduled for permanent removal within 30 days**
    - During those 30 days your account is deactivated but not yet deleted — if you change your mind, contact support before the 30 days are up to reinstate it
    - After 30 days, all personal data is permanently and irreversibly deleted in line with ZipDrop's Privacy Policy and UK GDPR requirements

    Any outstanding payments or active jobs must be resolved before your account can be deleted.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I forgot my password or lost access to my account — what do I do?">
    If you've forgotten your password, tap **Forgot Password** on the ZipDrop login screen. Enter the email address associated with your account and you'll receive a password reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive.

    If you've lost access to the email address on your account, or you're locked out for any other reason, contact the support team directly at [support@zipdrop.app](mailto:support@zipdrop.app). Include your full name and the mobile number registered to your account so the team can verify your identity and help you regain access securely.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
