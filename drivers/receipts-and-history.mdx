Every job you complete with ZipDrop is automatically saved to your account, giving you a full record of your breakdown history alongside itemised receipts for each service. Whether you need to check what you paid for a previous callout, download a receipt for a personal expense claim, or generate a VAT invoice for your business, everything you need is available directly in the app.

## Accessing Your Job History

To view your history, tap your profile photo in the top-right corner of the app and select **Job History**. Your requests are listed in reverse chronological order — most recent first — so your latest job is always at the top.

Each entry in your history displays:

- **Date and time** the request was made
- **Service type** (e.g. Battery Jump-Start, Flat Tyre Change)
- **Mechanic's name** and their star rating for that job
- **Total cost** charged, including any parts
- **Status** — Completed, Cancelled, or Disputed

## Viewing and Downloading a Receipt

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap the job in your history">
    From **Profile → Job History**, tap the job whose receipt you want to view. This opens the full job summary, including a breakdown of labour, parts, and any applicable surcharges.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap 'View Receipt'">
    Scroll to the bottom of the job summary and tap **View Receipt**. This opens a formatted, itemised receipt showing every charge associated with the job.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download or email your receipt">
    Tap **Download PDF** to save the receipt to your device, or tap **Email Receipt** to have it sent to the email address registered on your ZipDrop account. The PDF is formatted for printing and suitable for expense submissions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  ZipDrop automatically sends a receipt to your registered email address as soon as each job is marked complete. Check your inbox (and spam folder) if you need a receipt quickly and don't have access to the app.
</Note>

## Business Invoices

If you use ZipDrop for work purposes, you can switch to **Business Billing Mode** to receive full VAT invoices instead of standard receipts. VAT invoices include your company name, registered address, and VAT number, making them suitable for business expense claims and VAT reclaim submissions.

### Enabling Business Billing Mode

1. Go to **Profile → Account Settings → Billing**.
2. Toggle on **Business Billing Mode**.
3. Enter your **company name**, **registered address**, and **VAT number** (if applicable).
4. Tap **Save**.

Once enabled, all future jobs will generate VAT invoices automatically. You can also retrospectively request a VAT invoice for a previous job by tapping the job in your history and selecting **Request VAT Invoice** — ZipDrop will send it to your registered email within one business day.

<Tip>
  If your company has multiple drivers using ZipDrop, ask your account administrator to set up a **Business Account** so all jobs are consolidated under a single billing profile. Contact **business@zipdrop.co.uk** to get started.
</Tip>

## Disputing a Charge

If you notice a charge on a completed job that doesn't look right — whether that's an incorrect parts cost, a duplicate payment, or a service discrepancy — you can raise a dispute directly from your job history.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the job in your history">
    Go to **Profile → Job History** and tap the job you want to dispute.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap 'Report Issue'">
    Scroll to the bottom of the job summary and tap **Report Issue**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a reason and add details">
    Choose the reason that best describes the problem from the list provided (e.g. *Incorrect charge*, *Parts not used*, *Service not completed*). Add any additional context in the text field — the more detail you provide, the faster the team can resolve your case.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit and await a response">
    Tap **Submit**. The ZipDrop support team will review your dispute and respond within **24 hours**. You'll receive their response via in-app notification and email.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If your dispute is upheld, any refund due is returned to your original payment method within 3–5 business days.

## Exporting Your History as a CSV

If you manage your own expenses or need to submit a bulk record of ZipDrop jobs to your accounts team, you can export your entire job history as a CSV file.

To do this, go to **Profile → Job History** and tap the **Export** button in the top-right corner. ZipDrop will generate a CSV file containing all your jobs — including dates, service types, costs, and statuses — and send it to your registered email address within a few minutes.

<Tip>
  Use the CSV export at the end of each month to keep your expense records tidy. The file opens in Excel, Google Sheets, or any standard accounting tool, making it easy to categorise and submit your ZipDrop costs alongside other business expenses.
</Tip>
