ZipDrop is the fastest way to get roadside help when you need it most. Whether you've got a flat tyre on the motorway, a dead battery in a car park, or you've run out of fuel miles from the nearest petrol station, ZipDrop connects you with a verified, local mechanic or recovery professional in minutes — not hours. Simply open the app, describe your situation, and ZipDrop matches you with the nearest available expert who can get you back on the road.

## Why drivers choose ZipDrop

- **Instant matching** — ZipDrop's location-aware dispatch system finds the closest available professional and sends them your way immediately, so you're never left waiting longer than necessary.
- **Real-time tracking** — Once a mechanic accepts your job, you can follow their progress on a live map and see an accurate estimated arrival time, so you always know exactly what's happening.
- **Upfront pricing** — You see the full cost of the job before you confirm, with no hidden call-out fees or surprise charges. What you're quoted is what you pay.
- **24/7 availability** — Breakdowns don't keep business hours, and neither does ZipDrop. The service runs around the clock, every day of the year, including bank holidays.
- **Vetted professionals** — Every mechanic and recovery driver on the ZipDrop platform has been identity-checked, qualification-verified, and rated by other drivers, so you can trust who's turning up at your vehicle.
