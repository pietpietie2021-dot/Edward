The ZipDrop app is your direct line to roadside help, and downloading it takes less than a minute. The app is free to install on both iPhone and Android devices, and you only pay when you request a job. Having ZipDrop installed before you need it means that when a breakdown happens, you can get help on the way without any delay.

## Download for your device

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iOS (iPhone & iPad)">
    ### System requirements

    Your device must be running **iOS 14.0 or later**. ZipDrop is compatible with iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch. To check your iOS version, go to **Settings → General → About** and look at the Software Version field.

    ### Download steps

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the App Store">
        On your iPhone or iPad, tap the **App Store** icon on your home screen. Make sure you're signed in to your Apple ID — if you're not, go to **Settings → Sign in to your iPhone** first.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Search for ZipDrop">
        Tap the **Search** tab at the bottom of the App Store, then type **ZipDrop** into the search bar at the top of the screen and tap **Search** on your keyboard.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select the ZipDrop app">
        In the results, tap the ZipDrop listing — look for the official ZipDrop logo. Tap the app name to open its product page and confirm it is published by **ZipDrop Technologies Ltd**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap Get to install">
        Tap the **Get** button next to the app icon. You may be prompted to authenticate with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password. The app will download and install automatically.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open ZipDrop">
        Once the download is complete, tap **Open** in the App Store, or find the ZipDrop icon on your home screen and tap it to launch the app.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      When ZipDrop asks for permission to access your location, tap **Allow While Using App** or **Always Allow**. ZipDrop uses your location to find nearby mechanics and to share your exact breakdown position with the professional on their way to you — without it, the app cannot match you with help.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android">
    ### System requirements

    Your device must be running **Android 8.0 (Oreo) or later**. To check your Android version, go to **Settings → About Phone → Software Information** and look for the Android Version field.

    ### Download steps

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Google Play">
        On your Android device, tap the **Google Play** icon in your app drawer or on your home screen. Make sure you're signed in to a Google account — if not, tap **Sign in** and follow the prompts.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Search for ZipDrop">
        Tap the **search bar** at the top of the screen and type **ZipDrop**. Tap the search icon or press Enter on your keyboard to see results.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select the ZipDrop app">
        Tap the ZipDrop listing in the search results. On the app page, confirm the developer is listed as **ZipDrop Technologies Ltd** before proceeding.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap Install">
        Tap the **Install** button. Google Play will download and install the app automatically. You may be asked to accept permissions during or after installation.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open ZipDrop">
        Once installation is complete, tap **Open** in Google Play, or find ZipDrop in your app drawer and tap it to launch.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      When ZipDrop requests location access, tap **Allow all the time** or **Allow only while using the app**. Your location is essential for matching you with the nearest available professional and for guiding them directly to your breakdown location. If you accidentally deny permission, you can enable it later in **Settings → Apps → ZipDrop → Permissions → Location**.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
