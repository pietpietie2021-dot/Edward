Creating a ZipDrop account takes less than two minutes and gives you instant access to on-demand roadside assistance wherever you are. You'll need a mobile number or email address to sign up, and you'll add your vehicle details and a payment method as part of the process — that way, when a breakdown happens, you can request help immediately without stopping to fill in any forms.

## Set up your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open ZipDrop and tap 'Get Started'">
    Launch the ZipDrop app on your device. On the welcome screen, tap the **Get Started** button. If you already have an account, tap **Log In** instead — this step is for new users only.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your mobile number or email address">
    On the sign-up screen, choose how you'd like to register. Enter either your **mobile phone number** (including the country code, e.g. +44 for the UK) or your **email address**. ZipDrop uses this to identify your account and send you important job updates. Tap **Continue** when you're ready.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your identity with a one-time code">
    ZipDrop sends a **6-digit verification code** to the phone number or email address you entered. Check your messages or inbox, enter the code into the app, and tap **Verify**. The code expires after 10 minutes — if it expires before you enter it, tap **Resend code** to get a new one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your name and vehicle details">
    Enter your **first and last name**, then add your primary vehicle. You'll need the following details:

    - **Make** — the manufacturer of your vehicle (e.g. Ford, Toyota, BMW)
    - **Model** — the specific model (e.g. Focus, Yaris, 3 Series)
    - **Colour** — the main body colour of your vehicle
    - **Registration number** — your vehicle's number plate, exactly as it appears

    These details help mechanics identify your vehicle quickly when they arrive at the scene. Tap **Save Vehicle** when you're done.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a payment method">
    Add a **debit or credit card** to your account so you can pay for jobs seamlessly once they're complete. ZipDrop accepts Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. Your card details are encrypted and stored securely — ZipDrop never stores your full card number on its servers. You can also add Apple Pay or Google Pay on supported devices. Tap **Add Payment Method**, enter your details, and tap **Save**.

    Once your payment method is saved, your account is ready. Tap **Go to Home** to reach the main screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Make sure your vehicle details — especially the registration number, make, model, and colour — are accurate before you submit them. When a mechanic or recovery driver heads to your location, they use this information to identify your car in the field. Accurate details prevent confusion, especially in car parks, layby queues, or multi-vehicle incidents.
</Tip>

## Adding multiple vehicles to your account

If you own or regularly drive more than one vehicle, you can add multiple vehicles to a single ZipDrop account. There's no limit to the number of vehicles you can save.

To add another vehicle:

1. Tap the **Profile** icon in the bottom navigation bar.
2. Select **My Vehicles**.
3. Tap **Add a Vehicle** and enter the details for your additional vehicle.
4. Tap **Save Vehicle**.

When you request roadside assistance, ZipDrop asks which vehicle you need help with, so you can always choose the right one from your saved list. You can also edit or remove any vehicle from the **My Vehicles** screen at any time.

## Your data and privacy

When you create a ZipDrop account, ZipDrop collects your name, contact details, vehicle information, and payment data. This information is used to match you with the right professional, process payments for completed jobs, and send you job status notifications. ZipDrop does not sell your personal data to third parties. Your location is only accessed while you are actively using the app. You can review ZipDrop's full privacy policy within the app under **Profile → Privacy Policy**.
