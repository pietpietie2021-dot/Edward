The moment a mechanic accepts your request, ZipDrop puts you in control. You can watch them travel to you on a live map, see a countdown to their arrival, and contact them directly — all without leaving the app. There's no need to wonder where they are or when they'll show up.

## The live tracking map

After a mechanic accepts your job, the app switches automatically to the live tracking screen. Here you can see:

- **Your mechanic's live location** — a real-time pin showing exactly where they are on the map, updated every few seconds as they move.
- **ETA countdown** — a prominent timer at the top of the screen showing the estimated minutes until arrival. This updates dynamically based on traffic and your mechanic's actual route.
- **Route preview** — a highlighted line on the map showing the route your mechanic is taking to reach you, so you know they're heading the right way.
- **Mechanic profile** — your mechanic's name, photo, star rating, and vehicle details (make, model, and registration plate) are shown at the bottom of the screen so you know exactly who to expect.

## Sending a message to your mechanic

If you need to share extra information — such as a gate code, a change of location, or a detail about the vehicle — you can message your mechanic directly in the app.

Tap the **chat bubble icon** on the tracking screen to open the in-app chat. Type your message and tap **Send**. Your mechanic will see your message on their device and can reply when it's safe to do so. All messages are stored in the chat thread for the duration of your job.

<Note>
  In-app messaging and calling are completely free and work entirely within ZipDrop. You don't need to share your personal phone number with your mechanic, and they don't share theirs with you — all communication stays private and in-app.
</Note>

## Calling your mechanic

For more urgent communication, you can call your mechanic directly through the app without revealing your personal number.

Tap the **phone icon** on the tracking screen to start a call. ZipDrop connects you through an anonymised call bridge, so both parties can speak freely without exchanging private contact details. Calls are available from the moment your mechanic accepts the job until the job is marked as complete.

## What to do while you wait

Your safety is the priority while you wait for your mechanic to arrive. Follow these steps to keep yourself and other road users safe:

- **Switch on your hazard lights** as soon as you stop, even in daylight. This is a legal requirement in many situations and makes your vehicle visible to approaching traffic.
- **Stay in your vehicle if it's safe to do so.** On a motorway or fast dual carriageway, staying inside with your seatbelt on is usually safer than standing outside. Exit through the passenger side away from traffic if you must leave.
- **Move away from traffic if you leave the vehicle.** Step behind a barrier or move well onto the verge. Never stand in a live lane or directly behind your vehicle.
- **Do not attempt repairs on the carriageway.** Wait for your mechanic to arrive — they're equipped and trained to work safely at the roadside.
- **Keep your phone charged.** You'll need it to track your mechanic and receive notifications. If your battery is low, use a car charger or a power bank if you have one.

<Tip>
  While you wait, consider sharing your live location with a friend or family member. You can do this directly from ZipDrop by tapping **Share my location** on the tracking screen, or use your phone's built-in location sharing. It's a simple step that means someone you trust always knows where you are.
</Tip>

## If your mechanic is delayed

Traffic, road closures, or unexpected circumstances can occasionally push back an ETA. ZipDrop monitors your mechanic's progress automatically and will send you a notification if the estimated arrival time changes significantly.

If your mechanic is running late:

1. **Check the updated ETA** on the tracking screen — the countdown adjusts in real time and is usually the most accurate indicator.
2. **Send a message or call your mechanic** using the in-app chat or phone icon to ask for an update directly.
3. **Contact ZipDrop support** if your mechanic has stopped moving, is unreachable, or if the ETA has increased by more than 15 minutes without explanation. Tap **Help** in the bottom navigation bar and select **Issue with my current job** to reach the support team. Support is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

ZipDrop's support team can check on your mechanic's status, provide an updated ETA, or arrange a replacement mechanic if needed. You won't be left waiting without answers.
