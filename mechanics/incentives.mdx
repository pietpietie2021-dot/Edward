Beyond your per-job earnings, ZipDrop offers a range of bonuses and rewards designed to recognise consistent, high-quality work and help you maximise your income on the platform. From weekly challenges that reset every Monday to one-off milestone bonuses as your job count grows, there are multiple ways to earn more — and to unlock extra visibility and support as a top-performing mechanic.

## Types of incentives

### 1. Weekly Challenges

Each week, ZipDrop sets a completion challenge in your area. If you hit the target number of jobs within the Monday–Sunday window, you earn a bonus payment on top of your regular earnings. A typical challenge looks like:

> *Complete 10 jobs this week and earn an extra £30.*

Challenge targets and bonus amounts vary by region and week, so check the app each Monday to see what's on offer. Bonus payments are added to your weekly payout automatically when the challenge is confirmed at end of Sunday.

### 2. Peak Hour Multipliers

During periods of high demand, ZipDrop applies a **1.2x to 1.5x multiplier** to your base rate. When a multiplier is active in your area, a banner appears at the top of your home screen in the app showing the current rate and the estimated duration. You don't need to do anything to activate it — simply be online and accepting jobs during those windows.

### 3. Referral Bonus

Know another mechanic who would be a good fit for ZipDrop? Share your personal referral link from **Earnings** → **Referrals**. When the mechanic you referred completes their **first 10 jobs**, you receive a **£50 bonus** paid directly into your next weekly payout. There is no limit to the number of mechanics you can refer.

### 4. Milestone Rewards

ZipDrop marks your career progress with one-off cash bonuses at key job milestones:

| Milestone | Reward |
|---|---|
| 50 jobs completed | £25 bonus |
| 200 jobs completed | £100 bonus |
| 500 jobs completed | £300 bonus + ZipDrop Pro badge |

Milestone bonuses are paid automatically in your next weekly payout after the threshold is reached.

### 5. Top Rated Badge

If you maintain a **4.8 or above rating for four or more consecutive weeks**, ZipDrop awards you the **Top Rated badge**, which appears on your profile when drivers see job matches. Top Rated mechanics receive **priority placement in job matching**, meaning you're shown to drivers before other available mechanics in your area — leading to more job offers without needing to change how you work.

---

## How to view active incentives

Go to **Earnings** → **Incentives** in the app to see all currently active challenges, your progress towards milestones, your referral link and referral history, and any upcoming rewards. The page updates in real time as you complete jobs.

---

## ZipDrop Pro

**ZipDrop Pro** is the elite tier reserved for the highest-performing mechanics on the platform. You unlock Pro status by reaching the 500-job milestone while maintaining strong ratings and low cancellation rates. As a ZipDrop Pro mechanic, you receive:

- **Priority job matching** — you're offered jobs before non-Pro mechanics in your area
- **Dedicated support line** — direct access to a Pro-specific support team, bypassing the standard queue
- **Enhanced Pro badge** — a visually distinct badge on your profile that signals to drivers you're one of ZipDrop's most trusted mechanics

Your Pro status is reviewed monthly. If your rating drops below 4.5 or your cancellation rate rises significantly, ZipDrop will notify you and give you four weeks to recover before the status is reviewed.

<Tip>
  Check the **Incentives tab every Monday morning** when weekly challenges reset. Knowing the target at the start of the week lets you plan your availability around it — for instance, making sure you're online during peak hours mid-week if you're a few jobs short of the bonus threshold.
</Tip>
