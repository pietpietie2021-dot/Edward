Whether you're just getting started with ZipDrop or you've been on the platform for a while, there are always questions that come up. This page covers the most common ones from mechanics across three main areas: getting started, jobs and pay, and account management. If you can't find what you're looking for here, reach out to ZipDrop support via the **Help** section in your app.

## Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need formal qualifications to join?">
    Formal qualifications are recommended but not required for all job types. General roadside assistance and fuel delivery, for example, don't require specific certifications. However, **lockout jobs require certified training** in non-destructive entry, and **towing jobs may require a relevant licence** — such as an HGV or recovery operator licence — depending on the vehicles involved. When you set your specialisms during onboarding, ZipDrop will flag any certification requirements for each service type.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a joining fee?">
    No. Joining ZipDrop as a mechanic is completely free. ZipDrop only takes a commission when you earn — specifically a 20% service fee deducted from each completed job. There are no monthly fees, subscription charges, or upfront costs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I work for ZipDrop alongside other platforms?">
    Yes. ZipDrop does not have an exclusivity requirement. You're free to work for other roadside assistance platforms, run your own garage, or take private callouts at the same time. Simply go offline in the ZipDrop app when you're unavailable, and come back online whenever you're ready to take jobs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What equipment do I need?">
    The equipment you need depends on the specialisms you sign up for. After your application is approved, ZipDrop provides a **full kit checklist in the app** tailored to your chosen service types. This covers everything from jump leads and tyre inflation equipment to fuel containers and basic diagnostic tools. You're responsible for supplying and maintaining your own equipment.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Jobs and Pay

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What if a driver doesn't show up at their location?">
    Once you arrive at the job location, wait **5 minutes** and attempt to contact the driver via the in-app messaging system. If the driver doesn't respond and is still not present after 5 minutes, tap **Mark as Driver Not Present** in the job screen. You'll receive a **£10 callout fee** to cover your time and travel, and the job will be closed without any impact on your rating or cancellation rate.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if a job takes much longer than expected?">
    Some job types — particularly General Breakdown — include a built-in allowance for variable job duration, and your earnings reflect this. If a job is running significantly longer than the estimated time for reasons beyond your control (such as discovering a more complex fault), **contact ZipDrop support via the in-app Help button** while still on the job. The support team can review the situation and adjust your earnings accordingly. Don't wait until after the job is closed to raise time overruns.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I be tipped by drivers?">
    Yes. After a job is completed, drivers have the option to add a tip through the ZipDrop app. Tips are paid **in full directly to you** — ZipDrop does not take any deduction from tips. They're added to your earnings balance and included in your next weekly payout, or available for instant payout alongside your regular earnings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if a driver is abusive or threatening?">
    Your safety is ZipDrop's top priority. If a driver is verbally abusive, threatening, or makes you feel unsafe at any point, tap the **Safety button** in the app immediately. This alerts ZipDrop's safety team, who will attempt to contact both you and the driver. You are never required to continue a job if you feel unsafe — you can leave the scene and ZipDrop will handle the situation from there. ZipDrop operates a **zero-tolerance policy** towards abuse and will investigate every reported incident promptly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I update my service area?">
    Go to **Profile** → **Availability Zone** in the app. Use the radius slider to set your preferred distance — anywhere between 5 and 30 miles from your current location. Changes take effect immediately for new job offers. You can adjust this as often as you like, for example widening your zone during quiet periods and tightening it when you want to stay local.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I take a break from ZipDrop?">
    Simply go **offline** in the app by tapping your availability toggle on the home screen. There is **no penalty for inactivity** for up to **30 days**. If your account is inactive for more than 30 days, ZipDrop may send a check-in notification, but your account will remain open. If you need a longer break, you can also contact support to note it on your account. When you're ready to return, just go back online.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if my documents expire?">
    ZipDrop sends you a **reminder notification 30 days before any document on your profile is due to expire**. When you have the renewed document, upload it via **Profile** → **Documents**. If a critical document — such as your insurance certificate — expires without a renewed version being uploaded, ZipDrop will pause your account until it's updated. You won't receive job offers during that time, so it's worth uploading renewals as soon as you receive them rather than waiting until the deadline.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I close my mechanic account?">
    If you want to permanently close your ZipDrop mechanic account, go to **Profile** → **Settings** → **Close Account** and follow the confirmation steps. Any earnings already in your balance at the time of closure — including any jobs completed that week — will be paid out to your verified bank account **within 14 days**. Account closure is permanent, so if you think you might return, consider going offline or using the inactivity break option instead.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
