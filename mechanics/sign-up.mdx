Signing up as a ZipDrop mechanic or recovery professional takes just a few minutes. You'll download the ZipDrop Mechanic app, enter your details, choose your service specialisms, and upload your documents — then submit your application for review. There's no fee to register, and ZipDrop will never ask you to pay anything upfront. Once your application is submitted, the approval team will verify your credentials and get back to you within a few business days.

<Note>
  Signing up is completely free. ZipDrop never charges mechanics or recovery professionals to join the platform, and there are no ongoing subscription fees.
</Note>

## How to sign up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the ZipDrop Mechanic app">
    Search for **ZipDrop Mechanic** on the [App Store](https://apple.com/app-store) (iOS) or [Google Play](https://play.google.com) (Android) and install the app. Make sure you're downloading the **Mechanic** version — it's separate from the ZipDrop Driver app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap 'Register as a Provider'">
    Open the app and tap **Register as a Provider** on the welcome screen. If you already have a ZipDrop driver account, your credentials won't carry over — you'll need to create a separate mechanic account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your personal details">
    Fill in your:
    - **Full legal name** (as it appears on your ID)
    - **Email address** — you'll use this to log in and receive notifications
    - **Mobile phone number** — used for two-factor authentication and job alerts
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your service specialisms">
    Choose the types of jobs you're qualified and equipped to carry out. You can select one or more of the following:

    | Specialism | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Tyre** | Tyre changes, puncture repairs, and inflation |
    | **Battery** | Jump-starts and battery replacement |
    | **Lockout** | Vehicle entry assistance when keys are locked inside |
    | **Towing & Recovery** | Flatbed or tow-truck recovery to a garage or safe location |
    | **General Breakdown** | Fluid top-ups, minor roadside repairs, diagnostic assessment |

    You can update your specialisms later from your profile settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your qualifications and certifications">
    Upload clear, in-date documentation showing your professional credentials. Depending on your specialisms, this may include:
    - ATA (Automotive Technician Accreditation) certificate
    - IMI (Institute of the Motor Industry) qualification
    - RAC or AA approved repairer membership documentation
    - Any other relevant trade certifications or licences

    All documents must be **valid, current, and clearly legible**. Expired or unclear documents will delay your approval.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload a profile photo">
    Upload a recent, clear photo of yourself. Your profile photo is shown to drivers when you accept their job, so it must:
    - Clearly show your face
    - Have a plain or neutral background
    - Not include hats, sunglasses, or other items that obscure your features
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your vehicle details">
    Provide details for the vehicle you'll use when attending callouts:
    - **Make and model**
    - **Year of registration**
    - **Registration plate**
    - **Vehicle colour**

    If you operate more than one vehicle, enter the one you use most frequently. You can update your vehicle details later via your profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your application for review">
    Review all the information you've entered, then tap **Submit Application**. You'll receive a confirmation email and an in-app notification once your submission is received. The ZipDrop approval team will then begin verifying your documents and credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Upload documents as high-resolution images or PDFs in good lighting. Blurry, cropped, or partially obscured documents are the most common reason for approval delays — clear files get reviewed faster.
</Tip>

## What happens next?

After you submit, ZipDrop's approval team will review your application. This typically takes **3–5 business days**. You'll be notified by email and in-app if any additional information is needed. For a full breakdown of the approval process, see [Getting Approved](/mechanics/getting-approved).
