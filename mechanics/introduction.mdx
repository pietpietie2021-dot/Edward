ZipDrop connects drivers who've broken down with skilled mechanics and recovery professionals nearby — and you're the one who makes it happen. Whether you're an independent mechanic, a mobile tyre fitter, or a recovery specialist, ZipDrop lets you earn money on your own terms by responding to real breakdowns in your area. There's no fixed rota, no boss telling you where to be, and no upfront cost to join. You set your availability, accept the jobs that suit you, and get paid as soon as the work is done.

## Why join ZipDrop?

ZipDrop is built around the people who do the work. Here's what you get as a ZipDrop mechanic or recovery provider:

- **Flexible hours** — go online whenever you're ready to work and offline the moment you're done. You're in control of your schedule, whether that's early mornings, evenings, weekends, or full-time hours.
- **Competitive earnings** — ZipDrop's pricing is designed to reflect the true value of emergency roadside assistance. You keep the majority of every job fee, with transparent rates shown upfront.
- **Instant payment** — once a job is marked complete, payment is processed automatically and appears in your Earnings dashboard within 30 minutes. No waiting for invoices to be approved.
- **Full app support** — the ZipDrop Mechanic app gives you turn-by-turn navigation, in-app messaging, job history, earnings tracking, and direct access to support — everything you need in one place.
- **No subscription fee** — joining ZipDrop is completely free. You're never charged upfront, and there are no monthly platform fees.

## Eligibility requirements

Before you apply, make sure you meet ZipDrop's basic eligibility criteria:

- You are **18 years of age or older**
- You hold **valid mechanic, recovery, or trade qualifications** relevant to the services you want to offer
- You own or have access to the **tools and equipment** needed to carry out your chosen service types
- You have a **roadworthy vehicle** with valid MOT and insurance that covers **business or commercial use**
- You are able to pass a **DBS enhanced background check** (ZipDrop covers the cost)
