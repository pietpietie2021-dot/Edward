ZipDrop pays your earnings directly to your UK bank account on a weekly basis. Once your account is verified and your earnings exceed the minimum threshold, payments are processed automatically — no need to request them manually. This guide walks you through the full payout process, from setting up your bank account to understanding your payment history.

## Payout schedule

ZipDrop processes payouts every **Monday**, covering all earnings from the previous **Monday to Sunday**. For example, jobs completed between Monday 9 June and Sunday 15 June will be paid on Monday 23 June.

The **minimum payout threshold is £10**. If your earnings for a given week fall below this amount, the balance rolls over and combines with the following week's earnings until the threshold is reached.

<Note>
  Bank transfers typically take **1–3 business days** to appear in your account after ZipDrop processes the payment, depending on your bank. If Monday is a UK bank holiday, your payout will be processed on the next working day.
</Note>

## Setting up your bank account

Before you can receive payouts, you need to add and verify a UK bank account. ZipDrop confirms your account details using a small verification deposit.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Payout Settings">
    In the app, go to **Earnings** → **Payout Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your bank account">
    Tap **Add Bank Account** to open the bank details form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your details">
    Fill in your **account name**, **sort code**, and **account number** exactly as they appear on your bank statement.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for the verification deposit">
    ZipDrop sends a **1p verification deposit** to your account within 24 hours. Check your bank app or statement for a deposit labelled 'ZipDrop Verify'.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the deposit">
    Return to **Earnings** → **Payout Settings** and enter the exact deposit amount to complete verification. Your account is now active for payouts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Keep your bank details up to date at all times. If your account details are incorrect or your account is closed, ZipDrop cannot issue your payout and the funds will be held until valid details are provided. Update your details via **Earnings** → **Payout Settings** before your next payout cycle.
</Warning>

## Instant payout

If you need your earnings sooner than the weekly schedule, you can request an **instant payout** at any time for a flat fee of **£0.99**. The transfer arrives in your verified bank account within **1 hour** of the request.

To request an instant payout, go to **Earnings** → **Payout Settings** → **Instant Payout** and confirm the amount you want to transfer. You need a verified bank account to use this feature, and the £0.99 fee is deducted from the transferred amount.

## Payout history

You can view a full record of every payment ZipDrop has sent you by going to **Earnings** → **Payout History**. Each entry shows:

- The **transfer date** — when ZipDrop processed the payment
- The **amount** — total transferred after the service fee and any instant payout fees
- The **period covered** — the Monday-to-Sunday earnings window the payment relates to

Tap any entry to see the individual jobs that contributed to that payout.

## Tax and self-employment

As a ZipDrop mechanic, you are **self-employed**. ZipDrop does not deduct income tax or National Insurance contributions from your earnings — you are responsible for reporting your income and paying tax directly to HMRC via **Self Assessment**.

ZipDrop provides an **annual earnings summary** in the app each April, showing your total gross earnings and the total service fees deducted for the previous tax year. You can download this as a PDF from **Earnings** → **Tax Summary** to use when completing your Self Assessment return.

For guidance on registering as self-employed and filing your return, visit the [HMRC Self Assessment guide](https://www.gov.uk/self-assessment-tax-returns).
